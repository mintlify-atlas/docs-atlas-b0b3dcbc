## What is SentimentAPI?

SentimentAPI is an AI-powered sentiment analysis platform designed to help businesses understand customer feedback at scale. Process individual comments or upload thousands via CSV, get instant sentiment classifications with confidence scores, and export results for deeper analysis.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Log in to your SentimentAPI account to receive an authentication token
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze Comments">
    Submit single comments or upload CSV files containing thousands of customer feedback entries
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Results">
    Receive instant sentiment classifications (POSITIVE/NEGATIVE) with confidence percentages
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Statistics">
    Access your dashboard to see aggregate statistics and trends across all analyzed comments
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Data">
    Download your results as CSV files for reporting and further analysis
  </Step>
</Steps>
